Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard
1. Project Title / Headline

📈 Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard: Sales, Profit & Customer Insights Analysis

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze ecommerce sales performance, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, product category trends, and payment preferences. The dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor key KPIs and identify growth opportunities across regions, products, and customers.

2. Short Description / Purpose

The Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales and profit performance across different states, product categories, customers, and payment methods. It helps business managers and analysts track revenue trends, identify profitable product segments, and make data-driven decisions to improve overall business performance.

3. Tech Stack:
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

📊 Power BI Desktop – Primary data visualization and dashboard development platform.
📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Created calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic business metrics.
🔗 Data Modeling – Established relationships between sales, customer, product, and transaction datasets.
📈 Data Visualization – Implemented bar charts, donut charts, KPI cards, slicers, and trend analysis visuals.
📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

4. Data Source
Source: Sample Ecommerce Sales Dataset
The data is structured to support sales performance analysis, customer behavior analysis, and profitability tracking across multiple business dimensions.

5. Features / Highlights
• Business Problem
Ecommerce businesses generate thousands of transactions across multiple products, customers, and regions. However, identifying high-performing products, profitable regions, customer purchasing patterns, and seasonal trends can be challenging when analyzing raw transactional data.

Key business questions include:
Which states contribute the highest revenue?
Which product categories drive the most sales volume?
Which sub-categories generate the highest profits?
What are the preferred customer payment methods?
Which months perform best and worst in terms of profit?
Who are the top revenue-generating customers?

• Goal of the Dashboard
To provide a centralized and interactive analytics solution that:
Tracks overall business performance through key KPIs.
Monitors sales and profit trends over time.
Identifies top-performing customers and products.
Analyzes regional sales performance.
Supports strategic business decisions with actionable insights.
• Walkthrough of Key Visuals
📌 KPI Cards (Top Section)

Provides an instant overview of business performance:

Total Sales Amount: 438K
Total Profit: 37K
Total Quantity Sold: 5,615
Average Order Value (AOV): 121K

These KPIs help management quickly assess overall business health.

🗓️ Quarterly Filter (Top Center)

Interactive quarter slicer (Qtr 1–Qtr 4) allows users to analyze performance across specific quarters and compare seasonal business trends.

🌍 Sales by State (Bar Chart)

Displays revenue contribution by different states.

Key Insight:

Maharashtra generates the highest sales revenue.
Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh follow as major contributors.
Helps identify strong and weak regional markets.
🛍️ Quantity by Category (Donut Chart)

Shows product demand distribution across categories.

Key Insight:

Clothing contributes approximately 63% of total quantity sold.
Electronics accounts for around 21%.
Furniture contributes 17%.

This helps identify the most popular product categories.

📈 Profit by Month (Column Chart)

Visualizes monthly profit trends throughout the year.

Key Insight:

Strong profits observed during January, February, March, and November.
Negative profit periods appear in May, July, September, and December.
Useful for seasonal planning and promotional strategies.
👥 Sales by Customer (Bar Chart)

Highlights top customers based on sales amount.

Key Insight:

Harivansh and Madhav are among the highest revenue-generating customers.
Supports customer segmentation and loyalty program initiatives.
💳 Quantity by Payment Mode (Donut Chart)

Displays customer payment preferences.

Key Insight:

Cash on Delivery (COD) dominates with 44%.
UPI contributes 21%.
Debit Cards, Credit Cards, and EMI options make up the remaining transactions.

This helps optimize payment infrastructure and customer experience.

📦 Profit by Sub-Category (Horizontal Bar Chart)

Ranks product sub-categories based on generated profit.

Key Insight:

Printers generate the highest profit.
Bookcases and Sarees are also strong profit contributors.
Tables and Accessories contribute comparatively lower profits.
Useful for inventory planning and product portfolio optimization.

• Business Impact & Insights
📈 Revenue Optimization : Identify high-performing states and customers to focus marketing efforts and maximize revenue generation.
💰 Profitability Improvement : Understand which product sub-categories contribute the most profit and prioritize inventory investments accordingly.
🎯 Customer Retention : Recognize top customers and develop targeted loyalty programs to increase repeat purchases.
🛍️ Product Strategy : Leverage category demand insights to optimize product assortment and stock planning.
💳 Payment Experience Enhancement : Analyze customer payment preferences and improve payment options to reduce checkout friction.
📅 Seasonal Planning : Use monthly profit trends to prepare promotional campaigns and inventory strategies during low-performing periods.
